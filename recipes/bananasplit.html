Bananasplit

Ingredients

  • 2 Banana
  • 3 Scoop of Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Whipped Cream
  • Grapes
  • Sprinkles
  • Mint Leaves

Instructions

  1. Peel the banana and cut it in half. Place it on the sides of the bowl
  2. Add in grapes at the tip of the banana slice
  3. Add 3 Scoops of Ice Cream in between the banana slices
  4. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the ice cream and banana
  5. Spinkle chocolate spirnkles over the top of each scoop of ice cream
  6. Enjoy and eat before it melts
